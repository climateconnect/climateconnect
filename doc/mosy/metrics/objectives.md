# Key Objectives & Metrics

## Business Objectives
1.  **User Growth**: Increase registered verified users.
2.  **Organization Growth**: Increase registered organizations that share their projects/events/ideas and interact with users and other organizations. 
3.  **User Engagement**: Users become active in climate projects and organizations or start their own initiatives.
4.  **Project Engagement**: Increase the number of active projects and interactions with users (membership, likes, comments).
5.  **Global Reach**: Expand into new Location Hubs.

## Technical Metrics
1.  **API Latency**: Keep P95 response time under 200ms.
2.  **Email Deliverability**: Maintain >98% successful delivery rate via Mailjet.
3.  **Uptime**: 99.9% availability for Core API.
